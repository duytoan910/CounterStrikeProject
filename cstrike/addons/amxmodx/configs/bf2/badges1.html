Badge List and Requirements

Basic Knife Combat - 20% Knife damage returned as health
   • 2 Knife Kills in 1 Spawn/Round
Veteran Knife Combat - 40% Knife damage returned as health
   • 50 Global Knife Kills
Expert Knife Combat - 60% Knife damage returned as health
   • 3 Knife Kills in 1 Spawn/Round
   • 1 Knife HS Kills in same Spawn/Round
   • 100 Global Knife Kills

Basic Pistol Combat - 20% Chance of immobilizing your enemy
   • 3 Pistol Kills in 1 Spawn/Round
Veteran Pistol Combat - 25% Chance of immobilizing your enemy
   • 100 Global Pistol Kills
Expert Pistol Combat - 33% Chance of immobilizing your enemy
   • 4 Pistol Kills in 1 Spawn/Round
   • 2 Pistol HS Kills in same Spawn/Round
   • 200 Global Pistol Kills

Basic Assault Combat - +10 Extra health
   • 4 Kills in 1 Spawn/Round
Veteran Assault Combat - +20 Extra health
   • Accuracy of 25% or higher
Expert Assault Combat - +30 Extra health
   • 5 Kills in 1 Spawn/Round
   • 3 HS Kills in same Spawn/Round
   • 2000 Global Kills
