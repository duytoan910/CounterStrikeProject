Badge List and Requirements

Veteran Sniper Combat - 2/3 Chance of getting a free Scout
   • 100 Global Sniper Kills
Expert Sniper Combat - Free Scout every spawn
   • 4 Sniper Kills in 1 Spawn/Round
   • 1 Sniper HS Kills in same Spawn/Round
   • 200 Global Sniper Kills

   • 100 Global M249 Kills
Expert Support Combat - +6 Damage per M249 bullet
   • 4 M249 Kills in 1 Spawn/Round
   • 1 M249 HS Kills in same Spawn/Round
   • 200 Global M249 Kills

   • 100 Global Grenade Kills
